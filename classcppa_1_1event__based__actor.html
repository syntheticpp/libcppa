virtual void cppa::event_based_actor::quit ( std::uint32_t  reason = exit_reason::normal)
virtual

Finishes execution of this actor.

Causes this actor to send an exit message to all of its linked actors, sets its state to exited and finishes execution.

Parameters
reasonExit reason that will be send to linked actors and monitors.
Note
Throws actor_exited to unwind the stack when called in context-switching or thread-based actors.

Implements cppa::local_actor.
